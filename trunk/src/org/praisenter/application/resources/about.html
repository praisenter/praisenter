Praisenter is a free church presentation software package producing high quality Bible verse, song, and custom slides with image, video, audio and transition support. Take a look at the highlighted features for more details.

Praisenter is offered completely for free under the GPLv3 license. See the license for more details.

Visit http://www.praisenter.org/ for news and downloads.

If you need help check out the forum at http://forum.praisenter.org/.

You can always find the latest version to download at http://www.praisenter.org/download/.
